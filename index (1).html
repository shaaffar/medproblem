What this is

Why we built this

At The Clinic, McMaster's health incubator, we run health innovation bootcamps several times a year. We work with talented students and early-stage founders who are ready to build — but who often struggle to find real, validated problems to work on.

At the same time, hospitals, clinics, and research teams deal with unsolved problems every day. They rarely have a simple way to get those problems in front of people who could do something about them.

MedProblem is that bridge. Clinicians and researchers submit a need. We review it and post it here. Innovators find it, work on it through our bootcamp, and present their ideas at the end of the program. The strongest ones move forward.

There is no sales process, no IP transfer, and no obligation on either side. Just a shared interest in getting real problems to the right people.

How it works
Simple on both sides.
If you work in a clinical or research setting
1

Fill out a short form

Describe the need, the problem behind it, and why it matters. Plain language. About five minutes.

2

Choose your visibility

Public with your name, anonymized, or private. You decide what's shared and with whom.

3

Set your involvement level

Step back entirely, stay available for questions, or be open to a formal collaboration. All valid.

4

The Clinic handles matching

We review, post, and connect your problem with innovators. You hear from us only if there's a meaningful fit.

If you're an innovator or student
1

Browse real problems

Every need here came from a clinician or researcher. Read them like case briefs — not hypotheticals.

2

Express interest

See something worth working on? Hit "Express interest" on the card and register. No commitment yet.

3

Work on it through the bootcamp

Join The Clinic's next health innovation bootcamp. Get mentorship, structure, and time to build.

4

Present at the end

Top ideas from each cohort are presented to the group — sometimes to the clinicians who submitted the problem.

Submitting a problem
What we ask for
01

The need

One sentence. What does your environment need that it doesn't currently have?

02

The problem

What's actually happening, for whom, and where? Two or three sentences.

03

Why it matters

What happens if this stays unsolved? Who is affected, and how often?

04

Your involvement

Step back, stay available, or open to collaboration. Your choice.

05

Visibility

Public, anonymized, or private. We won't share anything you haven't approved.

06

Contact (optional)

Institution, role, and email. Only shared with matched teams, through The Clinic.

MedProblem is run by The Clinic, McMaster University's health and life science incubator. Every submission is reviewed before it appears here. Our role is to act as a neutral bridge — we don't own any problem or idea, and we don't connect anyone without both sides being comfortable.

Institutions and clinicians who share problems here are not entering a formal partnership or transferring IP. They're making a real need visible to people who are actively looking for meaningful problems to work on.

Questions? Reach us at theclinic@mcmaster.ca.
